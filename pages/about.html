WHO ARE WE

..

We are a company that specializes in precision machining, specifically in the areas of turning and milling, utilizing German CNC machines. Our primary focus is on the production of spare parts and mass production components for various industries.

..

With a strong presence in the European and Arab markets, we export to countries such as Italy, France, Turkey, and Morocco. Our competitive advantage lies in our ability to consistently deliver high levels of accuracy and superior surface finish.

..

Our team of highly experienced engineers and technicians are dedicated to ensuring the highest levels of quality in every aspect of our operations,achieved through the application of the most rigorous international quality standards.
